About Us

The car showroom management program is an administrative system that facilitates the purchase or import of cars, the distribution of data to the branches and linking them, and then the sale of cars through the company's car showrooms.

The system contains a complete sales cycle, starting from the response to the customer, the price offer, the sale order, then the invoice, receiving the car, and following up on after-sales services.

Learn More

Services

We have several services including:

Buy cars

Importing or buying cars with the ease of recording cars received from an Excel file, adding them to stores and transferring them to exhibitions

Selling cars

The system provides a screen to make a price offer and a screen to follow up on the available cars to facilitate the creation and reservation of a car sale order.

Linking with car service centers

A pre-sale inspection can be done from the service center system. The customer and vehicle data are automatically displayed in the service center system

Follow up on reserved and available cars

The system connects sales representatives, branches, and customers, so it is easy to verify car reservations and cars available for sale
